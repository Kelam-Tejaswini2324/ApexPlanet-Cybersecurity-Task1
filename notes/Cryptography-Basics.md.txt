# Cryptography Basics

## Symmetric Encryption

Uses the same key for encryption and decryption.

## Asymmetric Encryption

Uses a public key for encryption and a private key for decryption.

## Hashing

### MD5

Produces a fixed-length hash value.

### SHA-256

A stronger hashing algorithm commonly used for integrity verification.

## SSL/TLS

Protocols used to secure communication over the internet.

## Digital Certificates

Used to verify the identity of websites and establish trust.

Learning Outcome

Learned basic encryption, hashing, and secure communication concepts.
