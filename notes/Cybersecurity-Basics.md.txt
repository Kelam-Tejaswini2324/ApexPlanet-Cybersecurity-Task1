# Cybersecurity Basics

## CIA Triad

### Confidentiality

Ensures that information is accessible only to authorized users.

### Integrity

Ensures that data remains accurate and unaltered.

### Availability

Ensures systems and information remain accessible when needed.

## Common Threats

### Phishing

Fraudulent attempts to steal sensitive information.

### Malware

Malicious software designed to damage or compromise systems.

### DDoS

Distributed Denial of Service attack that overwhelms a system with traffic.

### SQL Injection

Attack that manipulates database queries through user input.

### Ransomware

Malware that encrypts files and demands payment for recovery.

## Attack Vectors

### Social Engineering

Manipulating people into revealing sensitive information.

### Insider Threats

Threats originating from authorized users within an organization.

### Wireless Attacks

Attacks targeting Wi-Fi and wireless communication systems.


Learning Outcome

Learned the core principles of cybersecurity and common cyber threats.
